Jake Vichnis has experience as a music production teacher at the Third Street Music School Settlement in New York, where he teaches students aged 9 to 17. He develops curriculum and provides individualized instruction in various aspects of music production.

Previously, he was a production assistant at Progressive Musik Group, contributing to Grammy-winning albums and providing technical support for artists. He also worked as a library technician at The City College of New York.

He holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Audio Technology from The City College of New York and is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Western Governors University.

This site showcases Jake’s developer work focused on finance, machine learning, and web technologies. This is a collection of his recent work — personal projects, coursework, and experiments.
